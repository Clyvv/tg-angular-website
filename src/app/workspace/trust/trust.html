DMH Trust Company (Private) Limited, is a limited liability company duly incorporated in accordance with the Company Laws of Zimbabwe. Its core business is to offer varied and wide ranging company secretarial services including but not limited to company formation (public, private and non – profit), company secretarial services, Private Business Corporation registration, foreign company registration, tax clearance, Value Added Tax (“VAT”) registration and Trade Marks registration.

DMH Trust opened its doors to the public on 1 October 2001. Since then it has increased its customer base and services considerably. The Company is a subsidiary of DMH Holdings (Private) Limited.

Offered Services

  • Company registration
  • Public companies
  • Private companies
  • Foreign companies
  • Companies Limited By Guarantee (Non-Profit making Companies):
  • Private Business Corporations
  • Sale of shelf companies

Corporate Services

  • Company secretaries (annual secretarial fee)
  • Preparation of CR 14 forms, CR 2 forms and Annual Returns
  • Preparation of CR 6 forms & CR 8 forms
  • Share Transfers (each)
  • Issuing of share certificates (each)
  • Updating the Register of Members, Directors, Secretaries etc.
  • Carrying out company searches at the Companies Office
  • Dissolution of companies
  • Redemption of shares
  • Filing of any changes to Private Business Corporations
  • Tax Clearance for Bank use
  • Copies from the Registrar of Companies
