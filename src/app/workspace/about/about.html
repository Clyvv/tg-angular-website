The law firm of GOVERE LAW CHAMBERS ATTORNEYS/LEGAL PRACTITIONERS (Hereinafter referred to as "GLC") has been in law practice in Zimbabwe since the 1st day of April 2011. It has since witnessed exponential and monumental growth from humble beginnings. It consists of the Founding and Managing Partner, Associate Partner(s) and Professional Assistants whose academic credentials and prowess are succinctly and aptly enumerated under the profiles section. Moreover, it has capable supporting professional accounting, clerical, secretarial and administration staff.

It is in good and sound standing with the regulatory body for lawyers and firms, the Law Society of Zimbabwe.

GLC provides a plethora of legal services and solutions, including but not limited to, legal opinion and advice and profeesional work in disputes or matters pertaining to contract law, delict law (or law of torts), corporate and commercial law work, mergers, demergers, acquisitions, Zimbabwe Stock Exchange listings, company reconstructions, major foreign investments (whether direct or indirect), Zimbabwe Investment Authority applications, Tariff and Competition Commission applications, Exchange Control applications, taxation matters, loans and leases, agreements of sale, debt collection and hiring and hire purchase agreements, mining, litigation, criminal matters, notarial work, immovable property and conveyancing (including property transfers and mortgage and notarial bonds), information technology, environmental compliance, good corporate governance issues, deceased estates, labour or employment law, patents, copyright and trade marks matters, aviation laws, rules and regulations, domestic, matrimonial or family matters.

It includes amongst its clients an avalanche of individuals, couples and humongous companies operating in Zimbabwe and beyond the boarders, statutory corporations, and international organizations and institutions. GLC has a central office strategically domiciled at 12th Floor Causeway Building, Corner 3rd Street / Central Avenue, Harare, Zimbabwe and utilizes the services of correspondent law firms all over Zimbabwe. GLC has intimate connections with other reputable law firms scattered all over the four corners of the earth. It has provided professional legal services to an avalanche of clients across the globe.

Vision: To become the most preferred legal services and solutions provider in Zimbabwe, regionally and globally.

Mission: To provide manifold top-notch legal services and solutions to multitudinous clients towards justice, judgment and equity.

TARIFF FOR LEGAL FEES

Legal fees are charged in terms of the prevailing Law Society of Zimbabwe General Tariff of Fees for Legal Practitioners dated February 2011 and/or in terms of the Legal Practitioners (Contingency Fee Agreements) Regulations - Statutory Instrument Number 154 of 2014. Copies of the hard and/or soft copies of the tariff can be furnished to the client upon request.
